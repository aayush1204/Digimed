About Us:
Who wants the burden of carrying so many documents physically to the doctor everytime? And what if you decide to change doctors, or happen to change doctors in the middle of your treatment? How would the new doctor know exactly what had happened and what was prescribed and what should be done next? DigiMed helps do just that. We have created a portal for not only patients but also doctors where they can digitally store all their prescriptions, diagnosis', suggestions, and important scans.

Through DigiMed, we aim to simplify the process of taking care of yourself. We help you organise everything till today, so you're well prepared in advance for your next appointment. Be it booking an appointment to checking details of an appointment several years ago, or checking the X-Ray image you just took to viewing an MRI scan that was captured last year, we will save you the trouble of carrying all these heavy folders and digitilise everything for you. So even if you've lost one document, it's essentially just one click away!
